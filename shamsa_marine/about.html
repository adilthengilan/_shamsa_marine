Our Story

Founded in Dubai with a vision to simplify marine registration processes, we have grown to become the region's most trusted maritime services provider. Our deep understanding of UAE maritime regulations and commitment to excellence has helped hundreds of vessel owners navigate the complex world of marine compliance.

From humble beginnings as a small consultancy, we have expanded our services to cover every aspect of marine registration, licensing, and safety. Our team of experienced professionals brings together decades of combined expertise in maritime law, vessel registration, and marine safety.

Today, we serve clients ranging from individual yacht owners to large commercial fleet operators, providing personalized service that meets the unique needs of each client while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and compliance.
